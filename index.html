About Me

Hello! My name is Alejandro Ferreyra, and I’m a student passionate about technology, logistics, and web development. I enjoy learning new tools, solving problems, and working on creative projects that make a difference.

I run a logistics company called EYRA SRL, where we help businesses simplify their operations — from warehousing and order preparation to transportation and deliveries.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked with several clients to improve their delivery times and organize their inventory more efficiently by implementing systems that give them better control and visibility.

I’m an organized, reliable, and hands-on person, and I enjoy working closely with clients to understand their needs and offer solutions that make their day-to-day operations easier.
